About Us

Founded by Muhammad Irfan, Rfae Design is committed to creating high-quality casual kurta designs that seamlessly combine style, comfort, and affordability. Our signature collection features 3/4-sleeve kurtas made from Toyobo Japan cotton—a premium fabric that is lightweight, breathable, and non-heating, ensuring maximum comfort for any occasion.

At Rfae Design, we believe that looking good shouldn't come at a high cost. That's why our kurtas are designed to be stylish, versatile, and budget-friendly. Perfect for daily wear or casual outings, they cater to modern individuals who appreciate elegance and simplicity.

Choose Rfae Design for clothing that reflects practicality, sophistication, and comfort—all crafted with care and passion by our founder, Muhammad Irfan.
